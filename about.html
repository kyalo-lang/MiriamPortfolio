Who I Am

Precision in Steel.
Clarity in Words.

I'm Miriam Munyoki — a civil engineer and professional Swahili–Kamba interpreter based in Kenya. My work lives at the intersection of two disciplines that both demand accuracy, attention to detail, and a deep respect for the people who depend on them.

As a civil engineer, I design and oversee infrastructure that communities rely on daily — from roads and drainage systems to residential and commercial structures. Every beam and column matters.

As a Swahili–Kamba interpreter, I bridge linguistic and cultural gaps in legal, medical, educational, and governmental settings — ensuring that language is never a barrier to access or justice.

My dual expertise means I bring both analytical rigor and cultural sensitivity to every project I undertake.
